Comfort & Convenience

The charger hellcat is a very comfortable sedan. You can walk in and at the click of a button, you can turn your car on with keyless ignition. There is a lot of room in the frontseat storage and overhead console with storage. There is climate control, but if you are still cold, warm up your hands with the heated steering wheel. The sterring wheel is made out of leather and simulated alloy. You also have transmission, cruise And audio controls on the steering wheel. The rear view camera helps people backup and if you are still nervous about hitting something, the hellcat has rear parking sensors.

Performance

A 2019 charger SRT Hellcat starts at $67,495 and has 707 horsepower. The hellcat can get up to 19 city / 30 highway. The Hellcat has 650 pounds of torque at 4800 rpm. The size of the engine is 6.2 liter V8. The charger has 16 valves. It is one of the fasted sedans in the world!

Safety

The charger is a very reliable and safe. Children will be very safe beacuse of the rear door child safety locks and the child seat anchors. If you go for a drive, you won't need to worry about people in your blind spots beacuse of the blind spot warning accident avoidance system. The post-collision safety system and emergency braking assist can be very helpful when your driving. You can also monitor your tire pressure.
